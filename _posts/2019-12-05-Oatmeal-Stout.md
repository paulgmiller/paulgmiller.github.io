## Malt
- 10 lbs 2 row pale
- 1 lb Flaked Oats
- 8 oz Crystal 120
- 8 oz Roasted Black Barley
- 8 oz chocolate malt
- 4 oz black patent
- 4 oz white wheat
- 4 oz carapils

## Hops
- Norther Brewer 1.5oz/11.8% - 60 min
- British kent Golding 1oz/2.9% -1 min

## Yeast
-  Imperial Pub Yeast

## Water
4.5 gallon mash at 153F for 60 min. 3.1 gallons spage 


## Notes
-  Whirlock table @ 5 minutes
