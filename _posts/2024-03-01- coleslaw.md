# Spicy & Tangy Red Cabbage Coleslaw

## Ingredients
### Slaw
- ½ head **red cabbage**, thinly sliced  
- 1 **carrot**, shredded  
- ½ **red onion**, thinly sliced  
- 2-3 slices **pickled habanero**, minced  
- 2 tbsp **pickling liquid** (from habaneros)  

### Dressing
- 2 tbsp **white vinegar**  
- 2 tbsp **olive oil**  
- 1 tbsp **Dijon mustard**  
- 1¼ tbsp **honey**  
- 1 tsp **celery seed**  
- ½ tsp **garlic powder**  
- ½ tsp **salt**  
- ¼ tsp **black pepper**
