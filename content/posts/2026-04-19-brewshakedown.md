# 🍺 Cascade West Coast Pale — Brew Day Plan (Anvil + Sparge, Adjusted)

## 📋 Targets
- OG: ~1.050  
- FG: ~1.010  
- ABV: ~5.2%  
- IBUs: ~50–55 (from 60 min addition)  
- Batch Size: 5 gallons  
- Yeast: BRY-97  

---

# 🌙 Night Before

## Water
- Strike: **6.5 gal**
- Sparge: **1.5–2 gal (separate)**
- Add: **½ Campden tablet (crushed)**

## Preheat
- Set Anvil to **158°F**

## Measure
- Gypsum: **~1.75–2 tsp**
- CaCl₂: **~0.5 tsp**

## Stage
- Grain ready
- Hops measured:
  - 2 oz (60 min) ✔ already used
  - 0.5 oz (5 min)
  - 1 oz (whirlpool)
  - 1 oz dry hop (early)
  - 1–1.5 oz dry hop (late)

---

# ☀️ Brew Day

## Mash (0:00)
- Add salts to strike water
- Mash in at **152°F**
- Hold **60 min**
- Stir once or twice

---

## Sparge (1:00)
- Heat sparge water to **170°F**
- Lift basket
- Slowly sparge **1.5–2 gal (10–15 min)**

---

## Boil (60 min)

### Already added:
- 2 oz Cascade @ 60 min

### Skip:
- ❌ No 30 min addition
- ❌ No 15 min addition

### At 5 min:
- Add **0.5 oz Cascade**
- Add Whirlfloc (optional)

---

## Flameout / Whirlpool

1. Turn off heat  
2. Cool to **~180°F**
3. Add **1 oz Cascade**
4. Stir gently once
5. Let steep **10–15 minutes**
6. Do NOT boil

---

## Chill
- Chill to **66–68°F**

---

## Transfer
- Transfer to conical
- Aerate well (shake/splash/O₂)

---

## Pitch Yeast
- Pitch BRY-97 at **~66°F**

---

# 🌡️ Fermentation

- Days 0–3: **66°F**
- Then rise to **68°F**
- Total: ~10–14 days

---

# 🌿 Dry Hop

## Day 3 (active fermentation)
- Add **1 oz Cascade**

## Day 7 (post fermentation)
- Add **1–1.5 oz Cascade**

---

# ❄️ Optional Cold Crash
- 35–40°F for 2–3 days

---

# 🍺 Kegging
- Transfer to keg
- Carbonate to **~2.4 vols CO₂**

---

# 🧠 Key Notes

- Do NOT remove early hops
- Skip mid-boil additions (already high IBUs)
- Whirlpool = steep at 180°F, not spinning
- Focus on aroma, not more bitterness
- Keep fermentation temps controlled early

---

# 🥇 Outcome

- Firm bitterness
- Bright Cascade aroma
- Clean, crisp West Coast pale
